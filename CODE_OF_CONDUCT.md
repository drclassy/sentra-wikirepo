# Code of Conduct

Sentra Wikirepo follows a simple standard: be precise, respectful, and careful
with healthcare context.

## Expected Behavior

- Review claims before publishing them.
- Treat clinical and regulatory language with care.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Protect private data and proprietary implementation details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Publishing PHI, secrets, or patient-identifiable material.
- Misrepresenting clinical validation, regulatory clearance, or partnerships.
- Harassing contributors or reviewers.
- Bypassing documented crown-jewel boundaries.

Report concerns to `drferdiiskandar@sentrahai.com`.
